    Erstad Pancakes

    Ingredients

    1 1/2 X

    Amount Ingredient
    210g (1 1/2) Cups of Flour
    12g (1 TB) Sugar
    15g (3 tsp) Baking Powder
    2g (3/4 tsp) Baking Soda
    5g (3/4 tsp) Salt
    2 Eggs
    300g (1 1/2 Cups) Buttermilk
    40g (3 TB) Oil

    2X

    Amount Ingredient
    2 Cups of Flour
    2 TB Sugar
    4 tsp Baking Powder
    1 tsp Baking Soda
    1 tsp Salt
    2 Eggs
    2 Cups Buttermilk
    4 TB Oil

    Method

    1. Mix dry ingredients
    2. Mix wet ingredients
    3. Combine
    4. Griddle at 350
